# Ingredients

## Slightly Sour
* 2 eggs
* 2 tbsp. soy sauce
* 2 tbsp. rice vinegar
* 1 tsp. sugar
* 6 tbsp. water

## Slightly Sweet
* 2 eggs
* 2 tbsp. soy sauce
* 2 tbsp. rice vinegar
* 3 tsp. sugar
* 6 tbsp. water

# Recipe

* Bring a pot of water to boil.
* Boil each eggs for 8 minutes.
* Immediately soak in an ice bath.
* Peel the soft-boiled eggs, then marinate in sauce overnight.
